About Us

We are a small cat cafe based in Dublin, serving coffee and baked goodies. We also host hangout sessions with the current kitty residences, you can book in to make a reservation. All profits go towards helping strays get back to good health and find forever homes.

We look forward to seeing you soon.



House rules

- We respectfully request that visitors leave their dogs at home.
- Catnip is strictly prohibited within the property, its not that kind of cafe.
- Please do not feed the cat inhabitants no matter how hard they may persuade you, they are well fed.
- No flash photography.
